The most fundamental difference between Grid and Flexbox is how they handle positioning. Grid is perfect for the two-dimensional layout of items on a web page or app, as it can manage both columns and rows at the same time. Flexbox is one-dimensional. It handles either a column or a row. A flexbox container expands its items to fill the available free space or it shrinks to prevent overflow
